Signature Dishes

The Sharing Platter

A happy meal for adults.

Created for conversations, connection, and the simple joy of sharing food, our Toast Office Sharing Platter brings people together around the table. Featuring crispy falafels, grilled Portuguese chicken strips, creamy hummus, cool tzatziki, and a fresh Mediterranean salad with roasted brinjal.

Made to share. Best enjoyed together.

Buttermilk Pancakes

Sometimes the simplest pleasures are the hardest to find.

Soft, fluffy buttermilk pancakes made the way you remember them, comforting, familiar, and quietly perfect. At Toast Office, we're here to bring back the nostalgic classics that never go out of style.

A little taste of simpler times

Steak Sandwich
