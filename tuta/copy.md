FAQ => 🤔 For Tutors & Creators

    Q: Who can become a tutor on Tuta?

        A: Certified teachers, professionals, university students, and top-performing Grade 12 graduates are all welcome. You need strong subject knowledge and must pass a short screening and ID verification process.

    Q: How do I earn money?

        A: You earn in two ways: Content-Based Earnings (80% of subscription revenue, distributed based on views of your content) and Live Session Earnings (you set your price for 1-on-1 or group sessions, Tuta takes a 20% commission).

    Q: What subjects can I teach?

        A: Any subject covered in the South African CAPS curriculum or common university courses. We especially encourage subjects where local, quality tutoring is scarce.

📚 For Learners & Parents

    Q: How much does a Tuta subscription cost?

        A: Subscriptions are affordable, ranging from R79–R199 per month, giving you unlimited access to a variety of video lessons, quizzes, and study materials across multiple subjects.

    Q: Is the content aligned with the South African curriculum?

        A: Yes, the majority of the content is created by local tutors and peer educators specifically to align with the CAPS curriculum and local university standards.

    Q: Can I get one-on-one help?

        A: Yes. In addition to on-demand video content, you can book live 1-on-1 or group sessions with tutors for personalized help, subject to the tutor's set pricing.
