For Tutors

Who can become a tutor?

Certified teachers, professionals, university students, and top Grade 12 graduates are welcome after a screening process.

How do I earn money?

Earn through subscription revenue (80% split) or set your own price for live 1-on-1 and group sessions.

What subjects can I teach?

Any subject in the South African CAPS curriculum or common university courses.

Yes, most content is created by local educators specifically for CAPS and local university standards.

Absolutely. You can book personalized live sessions directly with your favorite tutors.
